Privacy Policy for CoupleSpace Health Data

Last updated: June 13, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how CoupleSpace collects, uses, and shares your health and fitness data when you use our health tracking features.

2. Data We Collect

With your permission, we may collect the following types of health data through Health Connect:

  • Heart rate and related metrics
  • Step count and distance
  • Sleep data and patterns
  • Physical activity and exercise
  • Nutrition and hydration information

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your health data to:

  • Provide personalized health insights and recommendations
  • Track your fitness progress over time
  • Enable social features to share progress with your partner (with your explicit consent)
  • Improve our services and develop new features

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your health data. We only share your health data:

  • With your explicit consent
  • With service providers who help us operate our services (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your health data, including encryption and access controls.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your health data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw your consent at any time
  • Export your data

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@couplespace.app
